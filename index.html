Springtime in Michigan

Springtime in Ann Arbor is beautiful. Even though it can be chilly at times, it's nice to walk my dog Bacon.

Sometimes we walk around the neighborhood, other times we visit the woods or rivers like the Huron River Watertrail and Argo Cascades.
